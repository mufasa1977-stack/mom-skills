## Auto-Invoke Discipline

Fires when: production decision has >1 viable path AND cost-of-wrong > 50 credits / 30 min / 1 reroll; Tariq says "think harder/decide/evaluate/which/should we"; context contains "first principles/inversion/second-order/margin of safety/circle/compounding/probabilistic/trade-off"; new feedback ready to translate into skill; render queued and preflight about to fire.

**Process**: (1) state decision in one sentence; (2) list ≥3 candidate paths; (3) run each through ≥5 of the 30 models; (4) converge → execute + report rationale; (5) diverge → surface to Tariq with model-by-model breakdown.

## Worked Example: "v11 feels like B-roll. Polish or rebuild?"

- **First principles**: cinema = continuous diegetic scenes with character continuity; polish can't add that to 44-cut montage
- **Inversion**: how to make MORE B-roll? Polish disconnected clips. Therefore polish is wrong move
- **Second-order**: polish → Tariq might approve → precedent infects 10 productions
- **Margin of safety**: channel-brand risk >> 4× credit cost
- **Compounding**: rebuild teaches new canon, polish teaches nothing

**All 5 converge → rebuild.** Locked 2026-05-22.

## Anti-Patterns

- Man with a hammer (one model)
- Fox vs Hedgehog (Berlin): foxes outperform hedgehogs at prediction (Tetlock Superforecasting)
- Gell-Mann amnesia (Crichton 2002)
- Survivorship bias (Wald WWII)
- Narrative fallacy (Taleb 2007) — single 8M-view video isn't proof of formula
